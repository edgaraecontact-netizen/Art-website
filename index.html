Good to Know

Frequently Asked

Tap a question to expand · Full details in my Terms of Service.

How do I commission you?+
Hit the "Commission Me" button and fill out my request form with your idea, references, and what you'd like. I'll reply within 2–3 days with any follow-up questions and an exact quote — no commitment until you approve it.
Do I need perfect references?+
No! Rough descriptions, mood boards, or "like this character but…" are all fine. Turning messy ideas into a clear plan is part of my job — you'll approve a summary before I start.
How much does a commission cost?+
Base prices are in the table above — most pieces land between $20 and $200 depending on how much of the character and the finish level. Extra characters and detailed backgrounds add to the base, and you always get an exact itemized quote before anything starts.
How does payment work?+
50% deposit up front — that starts the work and holds your queue spot — and the rest before I send the final full-resolution files. Invoices via PayPal or Stripe.
How many revisions are included?+
Two revision rounds at the sketch stage, plus one round of small tweaks at the end. Bigger changes after sketch approval are quoted separately — I'll always tell you the cost before doing anything.
How long will it take?+
Typically 2–4 weeks depending on my queue and the piece. You'll get WIP updates at each major stage, and you approve the sketch before I render. Need it faster? Ask about rush pricing.
Can I use the art commercially?+
Personal-use pricing covers personal use — profiles, printing for yourself, sharing with credit. Commercial use (books, products, marketing, monetized content) needs a commercial license; just tell me what it's for and I'll include it in the quote. Book covers already include one.
Will you draw my idea?+
I draw OCs, portraits, character sheets, chibis, book covers, illustrations, and design work in my anime / semi-realistic style. I don't take animation, comic pages, or requests to imitate another artist's style. When in doubt, just ask!
Can I get a refund?+
Before work starts: yes, fully. After the sketch is approved, the deposit becomes non-refundable and covers completed stages. Full details in my Terms of Service.
