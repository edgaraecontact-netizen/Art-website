Terms of Service

Plain language, no fine print. By commissioning me, you agree to the following:

The process

  1. You send your idea + references (via my commission form) → I confirm a written summary and quote.
  2. 50% deposit paid → work begins and your queue spot is held.
  3. Sketch stage → you approve before I render. 2 revision rounds included here.
  4. Render stage → WIP update at the color/render milestone.
  5. Final preview (watermarked) → remaining payment → full-resolution files delivered.

Payment

  • 50% deposit up front; it becomes non-refundable once the sketch is approved. Remainder is due before final delivery.
  • Invoices via PayPal / Stripe. Prices in USD.

Revisions

  • Included: 2 rounds at the sketch stage, 1 minor round at final (small color/detail tweaks).
  • Changes to things already approved (new pose, redesign, added elements) are quoted as paid revisions or new work — always with the price stated before I proceed.
  • Please batch your changes into one message per round.

Timelines

  • Your estimated turnaround comes with your quote, and I'll keep you updated at each stage.
  • If you're unresponsive for 14+ days mid-project, your project pauses and rejoins the queue when you return.

Usage rights

  • You get (personal license): personal use of the finished piece — profiles, personal printing, sharing online with credit.
  • I keep the copyright and the right to show the work in my portfolio and social media (delayed on request for unreleased projects).
  • Commercial use (books, products, marketing, monetized content) requires a commercial license — included in book cover pricing, available as an add-on otherwise.
  • No AI training, no minting as NFTs, no claiming the artwork as your own creation, and no reselling the artwork itself without a license that says so.

Cancellation & refunds

  • You cancel before work starts: full refund.
  • You cancel mid-project: completed stages are kept/paid; the deposit is non-refundable after sketch approval.
  • I cancel (rare — emergencies): full refund of any unearned portion.

I reserve the right to decline

Any commission, including content outside my comfort zone or requests to imitate another artist's style.
